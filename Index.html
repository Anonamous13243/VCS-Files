The Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles is a Nickelodeon show premiered on September 28, 2012. It features four mutant turtles - Leonardo, Raphael, Donatello, and Michelangelo - fighting against a powerful assassin, the Shredder. The turtles hide in the shadows battling Shredder's army and strategizing how to defeat him. However, a greater threat looms over Earth in the form of the Kraang, tiny alien brains intent on transforming Earth into Dimension X. Set in New York City, the turtles reside in the sewers with their rat father, Splinter. Together, they are determined to protect their planet from any danger that comes their way.
