The Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles is a nickelodeon show that was created in sept.28, 2012. It includes 4 mutant turtle that are fighting a powerful assassin, named the shedder. The Turtles are Leonardo, Raphael, Donatello, and Michelangelo. They hide in the shadows fighting shredder's army and are figuring out a way to defeat him. However, a bigger threat is rising towards earth. They call themselves the Kraang, tiny alien brains that figure out a way to turn earth into a dimesion x. The show takes place in New York City, the turtles live in the sewers with there rat father, splinter. Together they are going to save their planet from any threat they face.
