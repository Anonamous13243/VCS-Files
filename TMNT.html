The Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles is a nickelodeon show that was created in sept.28, 2012. It includes 4 mutant turtle that are fighting a powerful assassin, named the shedder. The Turtles are Leonardo, Raphael, Donatello, and Michelangelo. They hide in the shadows fighting shredder's army and are figuring out a way to defeat him. However, a bigger threat is rising towards earth. They call themselves the Kraang, tiny alien brains that figure out a way to turn earth into a dimesion x. The show takes place in New York City, the turtles live in the sewers with there rat father, splinter. Together they are going to save their planet from any threat they face.


First is the blue leader, Leonardo. Leonardo is the leader of the tmnt and is an honorable ninja. He was chosen leader by splinter and takes pride in it. He is a smart, kind, honorable, and skilled ninja. He can be a bit frustrated with his team because most of the time they don't listen to what Leonardo says to them. They usually do the their own thing or something else. While leonardo knows how to swing the sword, he also knows ninjutsu, and has strategic planning. He also is friends with the mighty mutants, april o'neil, casey jones, and karai. Leonardo also was the one who killed the shredder and ended this war. He also help defeated the kraang after they turned earth into dimesion x. Leonardo can be a bit strict of his team, but is still a good, and noble fighter.


Up next is the hotheaded turtle, Raphael.Raphael is the red ninja that has an bad temper. He often gets annoyed pretty easily, but besides that, Raphael is a strong, stubborn, skilled, hotheaded, and rebellious ninja. He is the muscle of the TMNT team. He does get a bit jealous of leo becoming leader and thinks he cando a better job than him. Raphael does have a soft spot for the ones he cares about. Now is the brains of the group, Donatello. Donatello is an intelligent, kind, noble, and anxious at times. He is usually the one who has to come up with every solution, because of how smart he is. He get annoyed at that sometimes because it can be too much pressure for him and doesn't want to be the one responsible for it. He is the smartest of the group, whihc makes him the most reliable on inventions and gadgets to help in any situation. They may be good friends, but I think that would just be it for them.


Next is michelangelo. Michelangelo is the youngest turtle of the brothers. He is very goofy, silly, rebellious, creative , and kind. He is definitely the more playful ninja than the others. He can be resourceful at times when he really needs to be in order to save the people he cares about. He can be naive sometimes when he meets new people or people he already knows. He can pay attention to whatever interests him in way that is cool, but can be very reckless with what he plays with. Michelangelo knows how to fight with nunchuks, ninjutsu, intuition, creativity, and unpredictability. He wouldn't hesitate to try new things, even though he knows that they won't work. Michelangelo can be childish sometimes, but he has a big heart and loves the people or mutants he knows.


Now it is the old rat, Splinter AKA Hamato Yoshi. Splinter is an old mutated rat that live in the sewers with his sons: Leonardo, Raphael, Donatello, and Michelangelo. Splinter is an overprotective, paranoid, wise, strict father, who cares deeply about his kids. While splinter can be a bit stern about the decisions the turtles make sometimes, it's really for there own good. Splinter doesn't usually go out on adventures with his sons, because it's there destiny, not his. He can be a good teacher at times, but he mainly spents most of his time with Leo because he is the leader and wants to teach him everything he knows about leadership. Splinter knows Ninjutsu, wisdom, weaponry, healing, high intuition, and pressure point intuition. Splinter also has a daughter, Karai. He original name was miwa, but he was kidnapped by oroku saki AKA the shredder. Shredder held splinter's daughter captive for over 15 years, but karai found out the truth and came to live her true father. Splinter's rivalry with the shredder has gone from brothers to archenemies. Throughout the years they have competing with each other for a while. This all started with a girl, Tang shen. She was splinter's girlfriend, but was killed by the shredder by mistake. Splinter may have had a hard past, but is still focused with the things he cares about in the present.

Next is the vigilante, Casey jones. Casey is a reckless, stubborn, and arrogant. Casey became friends with the turtles especially raph who is his best friend. Now is the main antagonist of the series, the Shredder. Shredder is a dangerous fighter, who seeks revenge of hamato yoshi/splinter. He tirelessly attempts to kill splinter after what he has done to his life. Shredder is highly delusional, aggressive, petty, pretentious, and manipulative, bordering on a psychopathic and emotionless spectrum. He has a right-handed sidekick, tigerclaw. Who he found after his trip to japan. Shredder is working and hiring people to help him defeat spliner and the turtles. He also captured splinter's daughter karai, and raised her as her own. Shredder knows martial arts, immense strength, intelligence, stealth, and acrobatics. Shredder also works with the kraang to help him get what he wants, by cooperating with them and making deals with them. Shredder did accomplish his goal in killing splinter and has achieved with happiness. But after the death of splinter. He got killed by leonardo by decapitating his head.
